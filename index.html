    OUR HISTORY

    ProtonMC has been around for over 3 months now (we just opend for the public). What started off as a small community minecraft server has developed into something we couldn't even imagine.

    OUR STAFF

    The Proton MC staff team are friendly, dedicated, and always online to help everything run smoothly. Have any questions? Make sure to do /staff ingame to reach out!

    OUR SERVERS

    We have Towny, Factions (coming), and SkyBlock (coming) on our network, all with fully custom builds and useful perks and features. Join today at Protonmc.ml

    OUR GAMES

    SKYBLOCK

    Skyblock is a gamemode where you start on a floating island with minimal resources. Compete with others or join them and jump to the top of the leaderboards! Difficult challenges are set for you through your sky adventure, complete them for rewards! It takes perseverance, dedication, and effort to get to the top!

    FACTIONS

    Factions is a popular team-based, pvp orientated gamemode where you claim land, build a base with your friends, and raid other factions. We have several awesome features such as custom enchantments and a handy shop GUI. Compete with other factions and strive to become the top faction, winning a free store coupon, today!

    TOWNY

    Create or join a towny. Make a nation! We have a big towny server with Dynmap (coming soon). Become the top town/nation leader or a dictator! Siege other towns and nations
